Scavenger Hunts

Scavenger Hunts are a fun way for you to explore Atlantis. Here at The Blue Crystal, we offer weekly and daily hunts in Atlantis. Choose your difficulty, whether you want to participate as an individual or as a group, jump onto one of our frequently running submarine loops and have fun finding the answers around the beautiful Atlantis. Of course, we offer prizes to those that complete any scavenger hunt!





Prizes

Many prizes are to be won from Scavenger Hunts! We give out prizes for the first team and first teams to complete the weekly and daily hunts and also small prizes for all who complete any scavenger hunt. Some of our major prizes include:
  • Gift shop vouchers
  • A free meal at any of our restaurants
  • Gourmet food hampers
  • Private submarine tour of Atlantis
  • And many more!

The prize for completing today's daily scavenger hunt is a discounted marine tour.

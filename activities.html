Scavenger Hunts

Scavenger Hunts are a fun way for you to explore Atlantis. Here at The Blue Crystal, we offer weekly and daily hunts in Atlantis. Choose your difficulty, whether you want to participate as an individual or as a group, jump onto one of our frequently running submarine loops and have fun finding the answers around the beautiful Atlantis. Of course, we offer prizes to those that complete any scavenger hunt!

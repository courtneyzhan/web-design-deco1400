What do we have to offer?

We're glad you asked! Situated in the heart of Atlantis, The Blue Crystal Resort, offers an experience like no other.

Due to our unique location, we encourage our guests to explore the nearby city of Atlantis. We host weekly and daily scavenger hunts around the city, revealing the nooks and crannies of this ancient city and the beautiful sea creatures that call Atlantis their home.

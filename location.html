How to get here

Atlantis is an unusual location to reach for sure - it wasn't discovered until 50 years ago and even now it is difficult to reach by conventional means. Our resort is focused on allowing visitors to come marvel and the hidden depths and of Atlantis' glory.

We provide a submarine that travels to and from coastal cities of neighbouring countries France, Greece and Albania. There is no additional cost for this two-way travel, rather, it is included in your holiday package. If you have any difficulty in travelling by submarine, please contact us directly here, to arrange an alternate method of arrival.

What's nearby?

Of course, the beautiful city of Atlantis! The famous sunken city has much to explore, as the ruins now houses many sea creatures. We offer frequent 20 minute trips to and from the city from our resort every day via submarine.

We also offer marine life tours around the surrounding areas, which is a fun experience for all ages.

Should you be feeling land-sick, we also provide services to the nearby city of Syracuse in France, where you can spend a day or two exploring this beautiful French city.

Our resort itself contains a shopping district, two restaurants and a giftshop, as well as all the facilities you may require on holidays.
